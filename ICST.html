Welcome to ICST

The International College of Science and Technology (ICST) is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in Sri Lanka.

Founded in 2024, ICST has grown to become a leading center for science and technology education in the region, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional programs across various disciplines.

Internationally recognized faculty, and strong industry partnerships provide students with unparalleled opportunities for learning and career development.

Computing

The Bachelor of Applied IT is a unique three-year undergraduate degree programme offered by the SLTC Research University in collaboration with the Ministry of Sports and Youth Affairs. This programme, aimed at building the next generation of Sri Lankan IT professionals is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of Sri Lanka.
